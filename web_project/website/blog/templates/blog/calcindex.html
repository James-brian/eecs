Statistics and probability

Statistics is the analysis of a set of data obtained as a sample from a population inorder to determine the future trends based on the outcomes.From the sample obtained one can also deduce the characteristics of an entire population.

Probability is the prediction of future events and how likely they may occur. P(E) = number of favourable outcomes/number of possible outcomes.
